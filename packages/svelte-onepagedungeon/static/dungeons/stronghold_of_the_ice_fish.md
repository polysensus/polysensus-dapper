# Stronghold of the Ice Fish

_The stronghold is situated high in the Tori mountain peaks. Currently it is overrun with cats._

1. A broken chest containing some gold.
2. A rear entrance into the stronghold. A dying goblin, a key among his belongings.
3. A massive stone gate with a keyhole on the eastern wall.
4. A pool of dark water, teleports a person outside the stronghold when drank from.
